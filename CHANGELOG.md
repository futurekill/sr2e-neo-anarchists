## 0.1.0 — Street gear

Droppable street gear from *The Neo-Anarchists' Guide to Real Life* (FASA 7208),
render-verified — **35 items across 4 packs**:
- **na-weapons** (8): Killer Accessories holdouts — Fichetti Tiffani Needler,
  Barton Arms Bracer & Gun Cane, Morrissey Élan/Elite/Alta, Raecor Sting, Eichiro
  Hatamoto II.
- **na-armor** (16): designer armor clothing — Armanté, Mortimer of London,
  Vashon Island, Zoé (fractional layering ratings kept in notes).
- **na-gear** (8): Sharper Image gadgets — Whitelaw sunglasses, Corona display
  glasses, Fellini-Med breather, Stanley door alarm, Fujicorp remote ignition,
  DES PhoneSecure scanner, Toshiba white-noise generator, Doafone Lock-Out.
- **na-vehicles** (3): semiballistic / suborbital / HSCT transports.

Not yet imported (the book buries these in heavily art-laden pages): lifestyles
(coffin hotels, DocWagon contracts) and two holdouts (Fichetti Executive Action,
Yamaha Pulsar). The setting/flavour text is out of scope. Requires sr2e ≥ 0.10.0.
